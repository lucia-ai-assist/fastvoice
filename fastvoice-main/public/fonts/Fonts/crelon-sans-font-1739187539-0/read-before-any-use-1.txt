Crelon Sans is a striking, geometric typeface.

This bold and modern sans-serif font is built with square shapes and hard corners, giving it a strong and industrial look. The letters possess a condensed form, which makes it a good option to be used for headlines and logos. It could easily fit into a futuristic or high-tech context.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Mans Greback created this typeface with a vision of a sturdy and precise font, useful for various applications that demand a modern and robust appeal.
